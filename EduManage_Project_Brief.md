# EduManage Project Brief

## Project Vision
To empower educational institutions with a modern, intuitive, and feature-rich management platform that simplifies administrative workflows and fosters excellence in education.

## Core Objectives
1. **Efficiency**: Automate repetitive tasks and centralize school data.
2. **Transparency**: Real-time access to student performance for parents and educators.
3. **Scalability**: Support schools of all sizes, from small private schools to large educational networks.
4. **Security**: Ensure data privacy and secure access control.

## Target User Personas
- **Administrators**: Manage records, generate reports, and oversee school operations.
- **Teachers**: Manage classrooms, input grades, and track attendance.
- **Students**: View assignments, grades, and school announcements.
- **Parents**: Monitor student progress and manage fee payments.

## Key Modules & Features
### 1. Student Information System (SIS)
- Student enrollment and profiles.
- Class assignments and schedules.
- Medical and emergency contact info.

### 2. Academic Management
- Digital gradebook with weighted averages.
- Curriculm and lesson planning tools.
- Assignment submission and tracking.

### 3. Attendance & Enrollment
- Daily attendance tracking (contactless/biometric integration possible).
- Waitlist and enrollment pipeline management.

### 4. Financial Management
- Tuition fee invoicing and collection.
- Online payment gateway integration.
- Expense tracking and financial reporting.

### 5. Communication & Portals
- Integrated email and SMS announcements.
- Parent/Student portals for 24/7 access.
- Event calendar and school news feeds.

## Technology & Design
- **Frontend Architecture**: React-based modular UI.
- **Design System**: High-contrast, accessibility-focused, premium aesthetic.
- **Data Security**: Encrypted data storage and multi-factor authentication.
